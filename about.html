Hi! I'm Audrey, an aspiring music producer and sound designer. I enjoy making dance/pop/electronic music with and for other artists. Aside from music production, I've also gained significant interest in sound design elements for video games.


When I'm not working with sound design and music, I spend a lot of time appreciating fashion design and touching up on my sewing skills, as well as doing fun graphic makeup.


My goal is to continue to work and collaborate with more artists and producers, and to make music with/for other people. Aside from music production, I'd like to gain more experience with video game sound design and work on my audio implementation skills. Over the last year or so I've really come to enjoy collaborative environments and gaining insight into the various perspectives that are present with whatever project is being worked on.


I love working with new people and challenging myself to explore different genres of music as well as techniques for production. If you're interested in working together, please feel free to contact me using my contact page!
