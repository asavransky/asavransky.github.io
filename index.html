Audrey Savransky | Producer | Sound Designer

About Me

Audrey Savransky is currently in their last year at Berklee College of Music, completing a Bachelor of Music in Electronic Production and Design, with a specialization in Sound Design for Video Games. Audrey began experimenting with music production in her second semester at Berklee and instantly felt gravitated to the EPD major. After four years in attendance, Audrey plans to begin releasing projects that she had worked on during her final semester.

In addition, Audrey has been a dedicated performer and instrumentalist since 2007, performing at various private parties, local summers festivals, and events at significant venues such as the Rock 'N Roll Hall of Fame. While music production has been their main focus, she still performs with friends bands at the occasional Allston house show.
