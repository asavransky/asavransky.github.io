Nobody (ft. NIKOLE)

"Nobody" is one of two singles that I produced this semester for my EP-491 course (also known as a capstone project). This song was a collaborative effort between myself and Nikole Ortega, the vocalist featured on the track. We spent the entire course of the semester having various recording sessions and meetings to ensure that the final product was something that we were both happy with. This track wouldn't have been a complete success without the help of Omisha Chaitanya, who helped graciously with mixing and assisting with recording sessions. Thank you to both Niko and Omi for all of your hard work!
