Recent and upcoming releases

Nobody (ft. NIKOLE)

"Nobody" is one of two singles that I produced this semester for my EP-491 course (also known as a capstone project). This song was a collaborative effort between myself and Nikole Ortega, the song writer and vocalist featured on the track. We spent the entire course of the semester having various recording sessions and meetings to ensure that the final product was something that we were both happy with.


This track wouldn't have been a complete success without the help of Omisha Chaitanya, who helped graciously with mixing and assisting with recording sessions. Thank you to both Niko and Omi for all of your hard work!

One v. One (ft. Akira Oni)

"One V One" is the second single produced as a part of my capstone project for EP-491. Similarly to "Nobody", this song was a collaboration with my dear friend, Akira Oni over the same timeline. Akira assisted with production and song writing. In addition to the song itself, we worked with their partner, James Paolicelli to create a music video to go with.


We used full body tracking in VR to track our live in person motion with customized avatars, used in game cameras to record varioius clips and then edited them together to create an iconic video for an iconic song for the girls and gays. Huge thanks to both Akira and James for all your help and creativity!
